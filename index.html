Construction of shallow foundations Shallow foundations in dry and low-moisture soils are erected in open pits. During the preparatory period, the necessary equipment is brought in, geodetic works and site planning are carried out. Stage No. Order of work 1 At the first stage, a pit is developed with or without fastenings for dense soils. 2 At the second stage, a foundation cushion is arranged, which can be concreted in place or assembled from prefabricated blocks, cast at the joints. 3 At the final stage, after the foundation cushion is ready, the foundation pit is backfilled.

Pile foundations on driven reinforced concrete piles

Posted on 29 Jan 2021 by Admin   |   Filed under Techology, Ground

7 Com

In the construction of bridges, pile foundations on driven reinforced concrete piles, as a rule, use reinforced concrete prismatic piles with a section of 35x35 cm and 40x40 cm, a length of 6 ... 18 m.

Bottom supports for bored pile bridges (BNS) The foundations for bridges on bored piles (BNS) are constructed by installing boreholes in the ground and then filling them with reinforced concrete. In the world practice of BPS construction, they have found wide application at high loads and large depths of strong soils (up to 120 m).
